# distance-between-hope-and-help

The title reflects the strength that lives quietly in exhausted communities. It speaks to the people who carry the weight without recognition until the moment they finally stand up, shift the balance, and change the direction of their town. It hints at a story where resilience wakes from its long sleep.

Concept Summary:
The player steps into the role of a community coordinator in a rural town stretched thin by rising need, constant emergencies, and the quiet strain of human service work. Their workspace is inside a thrift shop filled with coffee stains, half working computers, and a daily stream of neighbors who need support that never fits neatly into a form.

The world changes depending on the player's emotional bandwidth. Calm moments open paths toward teamwork, innovation, and connection. Overwhelmed moments bend reality into something dreamlike and disorienting. Paperwork multiplies. Time slips. Decisions feel heavier. Every choice affects the town and the player's emotional state.

Winning is not the point. The goal is to stay aligned with personal values while navigating systems that often conflict with them. The player makes decisions about scarce resources, community politics, moral dilemmas, and the tension between helping others and protecting their own well being. The challenges feel familiar to anyone who has worked in community care or seen how fragile rural support systems can be. I plan on scaling this along with my skills growth in the Computer Science Program.
